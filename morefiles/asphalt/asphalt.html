About Asphalt

Asphalt is a popular racing game series known for its fast-paced arcade gameplay, stunning graphics, and a wide selection of cars and tracks. Race through city streets, perform stunts, and compete against players worldwide!

Features

  • High-speed arcade racing action
  • Dozens of licensed cars
  • Multiple game modes and events
  • Online multiplayer races
  • Customizable vehicles and upgrades
